PickFlick FAQ

How does PickFlick work?

PickFlick guides you through a simple four-step flow: mood → subscriptions → time → who. It surfaces a small set of strong options from your subscribed platforms (or all platforms if you choose None).

Is PickFlick free?

Yes. The MVP is free to use. No paid features are required for basic functionality.

What streaming services are supported?

The MVP currently includes popular platforms (Netflix, Disney+, Max, Hulu, Prime Video, Apple TV+, Peacock, Paramount+). You can toggle a None option to search across all platforms.

Where is PickFlick hosted?

In this project workspace as a local MVP. Public hosting can be added later.
